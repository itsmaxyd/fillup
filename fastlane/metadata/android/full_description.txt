FillUp is your ultimate companion for managing fuel costs and optimizing vehicle performance. Whether you're a daily commuter or a road trip enthusiast, this intuitive app helps you stay on top of your fuel spending and efficiency trends.

Key Features:
• Fuel Expense Tracking - Log every fuel purchase with detailed information including amount, cost, and odometer readings
• Efficiency Monitoring - Calculate and track your vehicle's fuel efficiency (km/l or mpg) over time
• Multiple Vehicle Support - Manage fuel data for multiple vehicles with different fuel types (Petrol, Diesel, CNG)
• Visual Reports - Beautiful charts and graphs showing spending trends, efficiency patterns, and summary statistics
• Current Fuel Prices - Auto-fetch latest fuel prices by city to help you plan purchases
• Local Storage - All data stored securely on your device with optional cloud backup
• Data Export - Export your fuel data as CSV for external analysis
• Offline Functionality - Works without internet connection for core features

Privacy First:
FillUp prioritizes your privacy. All fuel tracking data is stored locally on your device by default. No personal data is collected or shared without your explicit consent. The app uses HTTPS for secure network requests when fetching fuel prices.

Perfect for:
- Daily commuters tracking monthly fuel costs
- Fleet managers monitoring multiple vehicles
- Road trip planners optimizing fuel efficiency
- Anyone wanting to reduce fuel expenses
